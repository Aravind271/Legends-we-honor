    ACHIEVEMENT

  • TRANSFORMED THE TATA GROUP FROM A TRADITIONAL INDIAN COMPANY INTO A GLOBALLY RESPECTED MULTINATIONAL CONGLOMERATE.
  • UNDER HIS LEADERSHIP, TATA’S REVENUES GREW MORE THAN 40 TIMES, AND PROFITS OVER 50 TIMES FROM THE 1990S TO 2012.
  • SPEARHEADED MAJOR INTERNATIONAL ACQUISITIONS, WHICH WERE ONCE UNTHINKABLE: → TETLEY TEA (UK) IN 2000 – MADE TATA THE WORLD’S SECOND-LARGEST TEA COMPANY. → CORUS STEEL (UK) IN 2007 – ONE OF THE BIGGEST INDIAN TAKEOVERS IN EUROPE. →JAGUAR–LAND ROVER (UK) IN 2008 – A HISTORIC ACQUISITION, TURNING THE STRUGGLING BRANDS INTO GLOBAL SUCCESSES.
  • LAUNCHED TATA NANO IN 2008 — A CAR PRICED AT ₹1 LAKH (~$2000), AIMING TO MAKE VEHICLES AFFORDABLE FOR INDIAN MIDDLE-CLASS FAMILIES.
  • ADVOCATED INNOVATION WITH PURPOSE, BALANCING BUSINESS WITH SOCIETAL IMPACT.
  • TATA GROUP GAVE AWAY 66% OF ITS PROFITS TO CHARITABLE CAUSES THROUGH TATA TRUSTS.
  • PERSONALLY SUPPORTED EDUCATION, HEALTH, DISASTER RELIEF, AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP THROUGH DONATIONS AND MENTORSHIP.
  • BECAME AN EARLY ANGEL INVESTOR IN INDIAN STARTUPS LIKE OLA ELECTRIC, PAYTM, URBAN LADDER, AND LENSKART.
