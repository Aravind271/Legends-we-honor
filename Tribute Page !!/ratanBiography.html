    BIOGRAPHY

  • FULL NAME: RATAN NAVAL TATA
  • BORN: DECEMBER 28, 1937, IN BOMBAY (NOW MUMBAI), INDIA
  • FAMILY LINEAGE: HE BELONGS TO THE PROMINENT TATA FAMILY, BUT WAS RAISED BY HIS GRANDMOTHER AFTER HIS PARENTS DIVORCED WHEN HE WAS 10.
  • DESPITE BEING BORN INTO WEALTH, HE WAS TAUGHT DISCIPLINE, COMPASSION, AND HUMILITY FROM A YOUNG AGE.
  • HE DID HIS SCHOOLING IN MUMBAI BEFORE MOVING TO THE UNITED STATES.
  • STUDIED ARCHITECTURE AT CORNELL UNIVERSITY, AND LATER COMPLETED THE ADVANCED MANAGEMENT PROGRAM AT HARVARD BUSINESS SCHOOL.
  • THOUGH HE COULD’VE HAD A CUSHY JOB ABROAD, HE CHOSE TO RETURN TO INDIA TO WORK AT HIS FAMILY'S BUSINESS — STARTING FROM THE SHOP FLOORS AT TATA STEEL.
  • RATAN TATA IS KNOWN NOT JUST FOR BUSINESS, BUT FOR HIS ETHICAL LEADERSHIP, HUMANITY, AND VISION FOR INDIA.
  • THROUGHOUT HIS LIFE, HE MAINTAINED A LOW PROFILE, STAYED UNMARRIED, AND FOCUSED ON HIS WORK, VALUES, AND PHILANTHROPY.
  • TODAY, HE STANDS AS ONE OF INDIA’S MOST ADMIRED AND RESPECTED PERSONALITIES, BOTH IN THE BOARDROOM AND THE HEARTS OF THE PEOPLE.
